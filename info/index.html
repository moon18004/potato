미국에 학생 비자를 받고 대학교를 다녀야 하는 모든 외국인 학생은 ‘International Student’ 으로 학교 원서를 지원 하셔야 합니다. (영상 튜토리얼)

이 후 Admissions으로부터 임시 합격 (Conditional Acceptance)를 받으면 International Services로부터 I-20 원서 관련한 이메일을 받게 되는데, 만약 임시 합격 통보를 받은 후 1주일이 지나도 이메일을 받지 않았다면 International Services (international@byui.edu) 에게 문의 하시는 것을 추천 드립니다.

I-20 원서는 본인이 하기에 따라 짧게는 2주 길게는 몇달이 소모 될 수 있으니 부지런하게 신청을 하시는 것을 권장 드립니다. I-20 원서를 성공적으로 제출하신 뒤 Approval notice를 받게 되면 Admissions 에서 Questionnaire 과 Track Assignment에 관한 이메일을 받게 되는데 이 두 이메일을 다 수락 하셔야 BYU-Idaho 계정과 이메일을 만드실 수 있고 임시 합격이 아닌 완전한 합격을 받게 되시고 학생 계정을 만드실 수 있으십니다. 학생 계정을 만드셨다면 마지막 절차인 International Deposit을 내실 수 있습니다.

주로 계정을 만들고 24시간 후에 학교 시스템이 백업 되면 International Deposit을 내실 수 있고 International Services에서 입금이 확인 되면 I-20과 함께 다음으로 해야 하는 것들을 학생 이메일로 받게 됩니다.

이제 받으신 I-20를 가지고 U.S. Embassy에서 학생 비자 인터뷰 신청하시고 비자를 받으신 후 미국으로 오시면 되겠습니다.


All students with F-1 Student Visa are considered as international students when applying to BYU-Idaho through Admissions including transferring students. Other visa type students may contact Admissions at admissions@byui.edu for additional information. (Video Tutorial)

Once an international student applies through Admissions and gets conditionally accepted to BYU-Idaho, the student will receive an email from International Services regarding an I-20 application. If a student does not receive the email within 1 week after the conditional acceptance, it is recommended for the student to contact International Services at international@byui.edu regarding the I-20 Application.

Depends on how a student prepares the I-20 application, the process of applying for an I-20 may take from 2 weeks to many months. Therefore, it is highly recommended not to procrastinate his/her I-20 application. If he/she fills out everything on the I-20 application and gets an approval notice from International Services, he/she will receive emails from Admissions regarding his/her Questionnaire and Track Assignment. The student must accept the emails to create a student account for BYU-Idaho in order to make his/her International Student Deposit payment. It usually takes 24 hours for the school system to update student account information before he/she can access to his/her deposit account.

If everything on his/her I-20 application gets approved and International Services confirms the deposit payment has been made, he/she will receive an I-20 through his/her BYU-Idaho student email address with additional information for what to do next.

Now, they can apply for F-1 Student Visa interviews through the U.S. Embassy.

Canadian students do not need to set up an appointment with the U.S. Embassy to get F-1 Student Visas.

All BYU-Idaho students receive Track Assignments, and for international students, their track assignments will always be their first two semesters. For an example, if your first semester is Fall, your Track Assignment will be Fall/Winter; if your first semester is Spring, your Track Assignment will be Spring/Fall.

It is quite important to understand what Track Assignment and Full Enrollment are because these may give you either great stress or great advantage during your academic years.

Full Enrollment(Fully Enrolled): Regardless of your track assignment and while you are resigning in Rexburg or near Rexburg, if you are registered at least 12 credits with one online class and on-campus classes or if you are registered with at least 9 credits of on-campus classes and online classes to have at least 12 credits, you are considered to be fully enrolled. You may fill 12 credits with all on-campus classes to have a full enrollment.

Full Enrollment Examples)

  • At least 12 credits of on-campus classes
  • 8 credits of on-campus classes + 4 credits of one online class
  • 9 credits of on-campus classes + 3 credits of at least one online class

If you wish to be fully enrolled and take multiple online classes, you must be registered with at least 9 credits of on-campus classes.

After your second semester, your third semester is considered as an off-track, meaning an optional semester. (If your first two semesters were Spring/Fall, your off-track semester is Winter.) During your first off-track, you may have three different options to choose.

  1. Take a full break.
    1. Since you were fully enrolled for the past two consecutive semesters (first two semesters), you are not required to take classes. However, you are required to be fully enrolled for the next two consecutive semesters after you take an off-track.
    2. You do not need to stay in Rexburg during this semester.
  2. Take classes but not fully enrolled.
    1. You may take as many online classes as you want.
    2. You may also take some on-campus classes, but you must apply for an override at Admissions. (Override)
    3. Although you are taking classes, if you do not meet with the Full Enrollment requirements, you are not considered as fully enrolled.
    4. You are required to be fully enrolled for the next two consecutive semesters.
  3. Be fully enrolled.
    1. You may first need to apply for an override at Admissions and register classes to meet the Full Enrollment requirements.
    2. You may use next semester as an off-track if you like to. If you want to take a full break or partial break during your on-track semester, you need to request an Annual Break at International Services. (Annual Break Request)

You might catch while reading the option, yes, the qualification for an off-track is you must be fully enrolled for two consecutive semesters prior and after a semester you wish to take off. With using this logic, you are not technically changing your track assignment, but you can adjust which semester to take off.

You can check your registration date at Registration Dates & Deadlines, or you can log in to your BYU-Idaho account and check under ‘Registration' page.

Once you are checked in with International Services, you can start working for on campus jobs. If it is your first job on the campus, you are required to get a Social Security Number at SSN office in Idaho Falls. All required information what to bring for the SSN application will be given by Human Resources office and International Services.

It is important for you to check if your nation fits under Tax Treaty from the U.S. Government because if it is, your income will be tax free up to $2,000 of your income. You may need to apply for the tax treaty beginning of each year at Accounting Services.

Keeping working hours is important because it can affect your I-20 status. If you are fully enrolled, you can work up to 20 hours (part time), and if you are not fully enrolled (off-track, breaks between semesters, and Summer session), you can work more than 20 hours per week. Holidays within semesters do not count as breaks, such as Thanksgiving week.

You are not allowed work off-campus unless you have CPT I-20 during your school years.

몇몇 전공들을 제외하고 대부분의 전공들은 졸업 조건으로 인턴십을 해야합니다. 그리고 외국인 학생들이 하는 인턴십을 Curricular Practical Training (CPT)라고 부릅니다.

  • Company Letterhead
  • Job Title
  • Job Description
  • Start and End Date
  • Working Hours per Week
  • Employer Signature

대부분 전공들은 Required Internship이 하나만 있는데 본인이 첫 인턴십을 Full-time position (일주일에 20시간 이상 일을 할 경우)로 한다면 인턴십을 하는 학기는 수업을 듣지 않아도 Full enrollment로 쳐줍니다. 그리고 교회에서 하는 인턴십을 제외하고는 인턴십을 Full-time으로 하게 되더라도 학교에서 part-time으로 하실 수 있습니다.

인턴십 두 학기 연속으로 할 수 있나요?
  • Required Internship이 두개이고 인턴십을 해본적이 없다, 그렇다면 가능합니다.
  • Required Internship이 하나일 경우 두학기 연속으로 인턴십을 하실 수 없습니다. 하지만 여름 (Summer Session)은 학기가 아닌 세션이기 때문에 여름세션은 포함 시킬 수 있습니다. 그래서 봄학기 + 여름 또는 여름 + 가을학기 이런식으로 인턴십을 하실 수 있습니다.
Except for several majors, most majors require students to have at least one internship before their graduation. As international students, we often call internship as Curricular Practical Training (CPT).

To take an internship class, you must complete pre-requisite classes for the internship class, and you would want to check with your academic advisor or your academic curriculum to check the pre-requisites since each major has different pre-requisites.

When you meet the requirement for your internship class, you can request an internship through I-Plan if you have an internship offer from a company. At this process, you are required to submit an Employment (Job Offer) Letter, and it needs to include following:
  • Company letterhead
  • Job title
  • Job description
  • Internship start and end date
  • Working hours per week
  • Employer signature
If you do not have all six information from the above or if your job is not related to your major, your request will not be approved.

After you submit your internship request through iPlan, your Faculty Internship Coordinator, Internship Office, and International Services will give you updates on your request. If your internship request gets approved, you will be able to register your internship class, and then you will receive your CPT I-20 for your internship.

Most majors have one required internship, but some majors have two. If you are doing your required internship, you may do any semester that you want to if you are working more than 20 hours per week for the internship (full-time internship) because it will be considered as a full enrollment. It will not be counted as full enrollment even you work more than 20 hours per week if the internship is not required. If it is a part-time internship (working less than 20 hours per week), you may need to do it on your off-track semester or stay in Rexburg and take classes to be fully enrolled.

You are not allowed to do two consecutive internships unless you have two required internships. If you have one required internship and did it on Spring, you are not allowed to do another internship on Fall. However, since summer session is not a semester, you can either start your internship in Summer session as part of Fall semester internship class, or you can finish your internship in Summer session as part of Spring semester internship class.

FYI, you can do up to 3 full-time internship during your whole academic years.

If you have an acceptance letter from a different college/university within the U.S., you must submit Transfer Out Request Form Form.

If you are transferring to a different school outside of the U.S., you may submit Departure Request Form

결혼을 하게 된다면 Student Record and Registration 에서 개인정보를 업데이트 하시면 됩니다.

미국인과 결혼 할 경우 개인정보 업데이트 말고 따로 학교에서 해야할 건 없습니다. 영주권 신청 관련된 것은 Immigration Attorney에게 직접 물어보는것이 보다 정확하고 안전하기 때문에 해당 정보는 기입하지 않겠습니다.

같은 외국인 학생끼리 결혼을 하게 된다면 추가로 하실 일이 없습니다.

배우자가 한국에 있어서 결혼 후 미국에 같이 데리고 올 경우 International Services에서 Dependent Request를 작성해주셔야 합니다 (자녀도 포함). 작성을 하실때 은행잔고, bank statements, 를 제출하셔야 하는데 dependent 한 명당 $4,500를 추가적으로 보여주셔야 합니다.

  1. 본인이 교회 회원이실 경우 본인 1년 학교 생활비 $17,000에 $4,500 더해서 $21,500 을 보여주시면 됩니다.
작성 후 허가가 나온다면 F-2 I-20 (Dependent I-20)가 나오게 되는데 그 서류를 가지고 미국 비자를 발급 받으시고 미국에 오시면 됩니다.

Internal Revenue Service (미국 국세청)에 의한 정보를 간략하게 요약을 한다면 미국에서 수익을 만들지 않는다면 세금 신청서를 내실필요 없습니다. 하지만 그 외 어떤 경우에서의 수익을 발생하게 된다면 (은행 이자율은 제외) 세금 신청서를 국세청에 제출 하셔야 합니다. 수익이라 함은 아르바이트, taxable scholarship, 주식 및 코인 (미국 계정), 인턴십, 정규직, 등 있습니다.

또한 Select Health 즉 미국 정부지원 보험을 사용하실 경우 세금 신청을 하셔야 합니다.

자세한 정보: https://www.irs.gov/individuals/international-taxpayers/foreign-students-scholars-teachers-researchers-and-exchange-visitors

세금에 관한것은 전문 회계인에게 물어보시는것이 가장 안전합니다.

According to Internal Revenue Service (IRS), international students are not required to file their taxes if they did not make any income. In otherwise, you must file your tax. Income can be but not limited to taxable scholarship, on and off campus job, stocks, coins, etc.

If you are using SelectHealth (U.S. Government support insurance), you are required to file your tax.

You can use the following link to for additional information: https://www.irs.gov/individuals/international-taxpayers/foreign-students-scholars-teachers-researchers-and-exchange-visitors

It is recommended to ask tax professionals if you have any questions regarding filing your tax.
